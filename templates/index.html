About the Artist

A passionate music producer and composer dedicated to crafting immersive sound experiences across film, digital media, and independent music projects.

With experience in cinematic scoring, ambient sound design, and contemporary electronic production, the artist has contributed to a variety of creative works including short films, indie productions, and collaborative multimedia projects.

His work blends emotional storytelling with modern production techniques, creating soundscapes that enhance visual narratives and resonate deeply with audiences.

From film soundtracks to experimental releases, each project reflects a commitment to originality, atmosphere, and sonic craftsmanship.

Film Scoring

Composed and mixed the original score for the feature film Tasrufdar (Djinns of Kashmir) — presented by renowned filmmaker Sudhir Mishra and directed by Kapil Matoo.
